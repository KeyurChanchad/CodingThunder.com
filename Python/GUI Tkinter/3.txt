Tollywood actress Rashmika Mandanna took to social media recently to interact with fans in a fun Q&A session.
She was asked about everything from how she feels about being called the National Crush of India to her equation with Vijay Deverakonda and Allu Arjun.
The actress will soon be seen in Sukumar’s Pushpa, which will see Allu Arjun and Fahadh Faasil as her co-stars.
When she was asked, Aapko Allu Arjun sir kaise lagte hai mai uske liye pagal hu, I’m crazy for Allu Arjun, how do you feel about him. she replied, I love working with Allu Arjun sir.
