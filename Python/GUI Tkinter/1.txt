England vs India: Virat Kohli-led India are set to play a warm-up game from July 20 to 22 ahead of the five-Test series against England, said a report. India will play a team arranged by the England and Wales Cricket Board (ECB), the report said.
1983 Cricket World Cup winner Mohinder Amarnath praised Indian cricket team captain Virat Kohli, and compared him to Viv Richards and Ricky Ponting.
